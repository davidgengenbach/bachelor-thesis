3rd call for paper sinn und bedeutung 1998 3rd annual meet of the gesellschaft fuer semantik ( ' association for semantic ' ) the third annual meet of the gesellschaft fr semantik ( ' association for semantic ' ) will be hold at the university of leipzig , between december 11 - 13 , 1998 . the main purpose of the conference , sinn und bedeutung 1998 , be to provide a forum for present work in progress and receive feedback from other member of the semantics community . paper describe research contribution on any aspect of semantics be welcome for discussion . conference language will be german and english . continuously update information on sinn und bedeutung 1998 ( include accommodation , conference program , location etc . ) can be find at the website : http : / / www . uni-leipzig . de / ~ asw / sinn98 / index _ eg . htm invited speakers : peter gaerdenfor ( lund university , cognitive science kungshuset , lund ) : concept combination -
