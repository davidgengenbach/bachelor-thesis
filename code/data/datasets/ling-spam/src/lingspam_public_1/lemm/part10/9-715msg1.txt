Subject: dependency - base grammar at cole - acl

cole - acl ' 98 workshop " process of dependency - base grammar " program saturday , august 15 , 1998 universit de montral montral , canada this one-day workshop will be a meet point for researcher interest in the implementation of dependency - base grammar ( dgs ) . it will be a forum for present different theoretical approach to dgs , as well as strategy adopt for their implementation . although the focus of this workshop be on dgs , it also aim at establish connection between different approach to the model and implementation of grammatical phenomenon . nine papers have be select for presentation at the workshop . additionally , four " short " papers will be include in the worhsop proceedings . this be the pre-final program for the workshop . the schedule be tentative and may vary slightly . remember that you have to register for the workshop . information about registration can be find at the cole -
