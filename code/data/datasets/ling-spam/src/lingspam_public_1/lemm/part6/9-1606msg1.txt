Subject: second language learn theory , r . mitchell and f . myle

second language learning theories rosamond mitchell and florence myle , both at university of southampton this text present an accessible and concise overview of the most current theory and approach of second language learn . it provide an up-to - date introduction to the key concept and issue , as well as a brief history of this area of research . written for student come to the study of linguistics for the first time , the book cover a wide range of approach , include linguistic , cognitive , and social .
