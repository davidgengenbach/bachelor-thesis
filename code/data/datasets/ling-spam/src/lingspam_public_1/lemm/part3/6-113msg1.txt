Subject: eskimo snow and scottish rain

i do n't know about eskimo word for snow , but scottish gaelic have a special word " turadh " for when it stop rain ! compare : tha an t-uisge ann . be the water in it " it be rain " tha an turadh ann . be the dry-spell in it " it have stop rain . " also : rinn e turadh san fheasgar . made it a dry-spell in the afternoon " it stop rain ( for a while ) in the afternoon . kevin donnelly
