Biography

Dr. Wile is a Research Professor in the Software Sciences Division within USC / Information Sciences Institute. He received the Sc.B. degree from Brown University in Applied Mathematics in 1967 and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Carnegie Melon University in 1974.
