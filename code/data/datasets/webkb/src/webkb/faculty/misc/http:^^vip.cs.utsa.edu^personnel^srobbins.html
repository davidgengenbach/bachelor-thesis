Summary of Research

My research is in the areas of parallel architectures and algorithms, distributed computing, visualization, and auralization. I have received funding from Cray Research to develop alternative designs for parallel memories, the National Science Foundation to develop a concurrency-based approach to teaching operating systems, and the San Antonio Area Foundation to develop new methods for understanding using visualization techniques.
