I received the B.Tech degree in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi in 1986 and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from State University of New York at Stony Brook in 1989 and 1991 respectively. Since 1991, I have been an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computing and Information Sciences at Kansas State University.
