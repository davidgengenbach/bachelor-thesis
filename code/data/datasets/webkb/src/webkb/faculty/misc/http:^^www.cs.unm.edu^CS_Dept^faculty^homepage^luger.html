Professor George F. Luger

Professor George F. Luger obtained a Ph.D. from the University of Pennsylvania in 1973. His specialty is artificial intelligence, especially computational models of human problem solving. He received a Burlington Foundation Award in May 1993. His special interests are artificial intelligence, computational models of human problem solving and knowledge engineering. He is coauthor of a popular Artificial Intelligence textbook and recently completed a textbook on Cognitive Science.
