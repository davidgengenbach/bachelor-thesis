Department of Computer Science

University of Idaho

Axel W. Krings, Ph.D.

Ph.D., M.S. University of Nebraska at Lincoln, Computer Science, -- Dipl.Ing. FH Aachen, Germany, Electrical Engineering.


Dr. Krings is an assistant professor of Computer Engineering and Computer Science and has been at the University of Idaho since 1995. He is also a member of the Microelectronics Research Center (MRC) and the Laboratory for Applied Logic (LAL).

His research interests include:

    fault-tolerance
    scheduling theory
    parallel and distributed systems
    computer architecture
    real-time systems
    Pointers to Research Areas.

Dr. Krings teaches classes in fault tolerant system design, scheduling theory, data communication systems, programming languages and digital logic.
