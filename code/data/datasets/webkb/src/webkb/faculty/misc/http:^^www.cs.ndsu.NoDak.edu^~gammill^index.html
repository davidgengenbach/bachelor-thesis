Earned his PhD in 1969 from MIT, taught at the University of Colorado, then worked for the Rand Corpation and has been at NDSU since 1978. He has a joint appointment with the Computer Engineering option in the EE Department.

Teaching interests include computer networks, robotics, operating systems, assembly language, and the C and C++ programming languages.

Interests include music, home automation, graphics and UNIX (tm) operating systems. He also enjoys bicycling, hiking, and fishing.
