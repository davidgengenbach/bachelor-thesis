Karen Van Houten

Department of Computer Science

University of Idaho

Karen Van Houten is an assistant professor of computer science and has been at the University of Idaho since 1969. She has been an assistant professor since 1980, when she received her PhD in Electrical Engineering. Prior to that, she worked for Computer Services at the U of I and as an instructor in EE.

The courses she is currently teaching are:

  • CS 213 Data Structures
  • CS 241 Computer Organization
