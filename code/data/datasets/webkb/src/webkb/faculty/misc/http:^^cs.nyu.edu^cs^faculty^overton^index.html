Interests

Numerical algorithms, their analysis, and related issues, primarily in the areas of optimization and linear algebra. I am especially interested in the analysis of eigenvalues, which arise in many different areas of applied mathematics. For example, recent special interests include semidefinite programming and stability of Hamiltonian systems.
