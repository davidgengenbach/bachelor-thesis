RESEARCH

Rule-Based Optimization

I am developing a rule-based query optimization environment. Innovations include the application of rules to more kinds of optimizer decisions than is usual and encapsulations of rules into modules with support for flexible intermodule control.

Collaborations Between Query Optimizers and Query-Plan Evaluators

Polymorphic data types and dynamic binding challenge the capabilities of conventional query optimizers. In this research I am exploring ways for optimization decisions to migrate back and forth between compile-time and run-time. I am supported by a National Science Foundation Research Initiation Award for 1992-1994 to explore this area.
