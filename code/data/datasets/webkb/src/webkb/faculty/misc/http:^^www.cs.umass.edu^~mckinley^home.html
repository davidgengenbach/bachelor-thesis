Research Summary

My main research focus is on developing compiler algorithms and tools that enable programmers to use a high-level programming style and modern languages, and yet still achieve high performance on scalar, parallel, distributed, and heterogeneous architectures. I am also interested in developing scalable parallel and distributed applications. For more details, click here.
