Data Structures and Programming for Advanced Placement First-Year Students

(Computer Science 19)

Times: 96F, 97F: 10
Instructors: Rus
Dist: TAS

This course is designed for first-year students who have had a substantial amount of computer science in secondary school. It is open only to first-year students who have received advanced placement credit for Computer Science 5, or who receive credit for Computer Science 5 on the department¹s placement exam given during orientation. The course begins with a review of the most important concepts covered in Computer Science 5, and then covers the same material as Computer Science 15.
