CS 470/570 Artificial Intelligence


CS J470/J570 Artificial Intelligence (3 cr). Concepts and techniques involved in artificial intelligence, Lisp, goal-directed searching, history trees, inductive and deductive reasoning, natural language processing, and learning. Extra research papers required for credit in 570. Prereq: CS 213, upper division standing, or permission.
