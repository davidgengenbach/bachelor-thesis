C599: Teaching in Computer Science

Computer Science Department

Syllabus for Fall Semester, 1955

The Seminar on Teaching in Computer Science for Associate Instructors will meet on Thursday evenings from 7:30 p.m. to 8:45 p.m. in Lindley 102 during the Fall Semester of 1995. The first two meetings scheduled for August 22 and August 25 will meet from 9:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. also in LH102. This seminar is required for all new Associate Instructors who have not taught in the Computer Science Department at Indiana University before and for all students who taught last year but did not have a chance to attend the seminar. You should officially enroll in this seminar. All students and faculty of the Computer Science Department are invited to attend any sessions of this seminar.
