    CS51 Project Book

    The Computer Science 51 Project Book is an informal text book for the course maintained by volunteers.

    This is a compressed version of the postscript file. Feel free to download it and print it in the science center. However, we recommend that you purchase a printed and bound copy from the SourceBook Center in the basement of the Science Center. I tried printing this once and it took hours. Plus, I had to hole punch the hardcopy myself- that ate up an afternoon! My book was also twice as bulky because I did not want to fiddle with printing double sided. Make life easy: buy the book!
