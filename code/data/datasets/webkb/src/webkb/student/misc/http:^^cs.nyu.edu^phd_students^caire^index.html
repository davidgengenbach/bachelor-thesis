Up and Coming Projects

Patrice Caire was born in France, and currently lives and works in New York City. She studied graphics and fine arts in Paris and Vienna, and later broadened her education in Germany and the United States. She launched a course in Virtual Reality at New York University, and is currently working on her Ph. D. in the Dept. of Computer Science.

Patrice was a member of the Siggraph 95 Panel on AESTHETICS & TOOLS IN THE VIRTUAL ENVIRONMENT. See the article in the NYU Electronic Journal of Sciences and Arts.
