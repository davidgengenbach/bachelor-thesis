Ian C. Westmacott

I am a graduate student in the computer science department at Boston University. My research interests are in language development and type theory.

In addition, I work as a technical editor and columnist for Computer Publishing Group.

Recent Academic Work

  • The Church Project. The incremental design and implementation of a typed functional language, based on intersection types. I am currently working on static memory management and lifetime-based optimizations in the compiler back-end.

  • A -Calculus Interpreter in Standard ML At the moment I am working on adding various typing systems to the interpreter, and investigating various parallelizing graph reduction strategies in the reduction engine.
