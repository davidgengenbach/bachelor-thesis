David Brogan


I am a Ph.D. student in the Graphics, Visualization, and Usability Center in the College of Computing. I work in the Animation Lab here at Georgia Tech. My current research includes creating dynamic simulations and developing Group Behaviors for Systems with Significant Dynamics.
