ABOUT MYSELF

I am a Ph.D candidate in Computer Engineering & Science at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU) in Cleveland, Ohio.

I'm developing software for Agile Manufacturing in C++ on VxWorks Real-Time Operating Sytem.


EDUCATION

  • 1994: MS in Computer Engineering and Science from Case Western Reserve University
  • Present: Ph.D candidate in Computer Engineering and Science from Case Western Reserve University

  • 1991: MIS Consultant at Andersen Consulting in Seoul, Korea
