Christopher D. Carothers

Chris Carothers a PhD student in the College of Computing at the Georgia Institute of Technology. He is a student of Dr. Richard Fujimoto. A PostScript vita is available. His interests include Parallel and Distributed Simulation and the modeling of wireless communication networks.

Publications

  • Christopher D. Carothers and Richard M. Fujimoto, Background Execution of Time Warp Programs, To appear in the 10th Workshop on Parallel and Distributed Simulation (PADS '96), May 1996.

  • Christopher D. Carothers and Richard M. Fujimoto, Distributed Simulation: An Effective Modeling Tool for Large-Scale PCS Networks, To appear in the Internation Journal in Computer Simulation (IJCS), 3rd Quarter 1996.

  • Christopher D. Carothers, Richard M. Fujimoto and Yi-Bing Lin, Simulating Population-Dependent PCS Network Models Using Time Warp, Proceedings of the 1995 Winter Simulation Conference (WSC '95), December 1995, pages 555-562.

  • Christopher D. Carothers, Richard M. Fujimoto and Yi-Bing Lin, A Case Study in Simulating PCS Networks Using Time Warp, Proceedings of the 9th Workshop on Parallel and Distributed Simulation (PADS '95), June 1995, pages 87-94.
