Brief Biography

I received both my M.S. and B.S. in Computer Science in 1990 and 1988 respectively, at Zhejiang University. From March, 1991 to May, 1994, I served as an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science, at Zhejiang University. Currently, I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences , New York University.
