Research Interests

  • Right now, I am working on a simulator for complex adaptive systems. In its current incarnation it simulates a simple market. Since it is implemented in Java, I have the alpha version on-line.
  • My longer range goal is the application of market ideas to the design and analysis of systems in several areas of computer science including intelligent agents, distributed systems, and networks. For example, I plan to use my simulator to explore credit based control strategies for ATMs.
