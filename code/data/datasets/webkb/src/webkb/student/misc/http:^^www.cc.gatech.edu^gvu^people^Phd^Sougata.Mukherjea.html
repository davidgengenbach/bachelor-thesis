Sougata Mukherjea

Sougata Mukherjea finished his PhD from the Graphics, Visualization, and Usability Center of the College of Computing at the Georgia Institute of Technology in 1995. His research advisor was Prof Jim Foley. At present, he is working in the C&C Research Lab of NEC.

Research

His research interests include Scientific, Software and Information Visualization. His dissertation was concerned with developing strategies for efficient visualization of the information space of hypermedia systems. He developed a tool called the Navigational View Builder for letting the designer develop effective views of the world-wide-web. Before this, he was involved in developing a visual debugger called Lens . He was also part of the team that developed Glyphmaker .
