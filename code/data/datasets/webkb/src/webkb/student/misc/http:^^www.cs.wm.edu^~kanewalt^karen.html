Educational Background

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at The College of William and Mary .

I received my M.S. in Computer Science from The College of William and Mary in May 1996. My master's research involved an area of cryptography known as Zero-Knowledge Proofs.

I got my B.S. in Math from Allegheny College , which no one has ever heard of, but I assure you it's a lovely little college in Pennsylvania.
