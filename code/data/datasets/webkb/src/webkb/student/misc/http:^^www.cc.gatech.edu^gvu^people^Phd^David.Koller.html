David Koller

David Koller is a research assistant at the Graphics, Visualization, and Usability Center in the College of Computing at the Georgia Institute of Technology (which was recently rated the top computer science graphics and HCI graduate program in the nation!). He is involved with the Virtual Environments Research Group , and is currently working on a project integrating GIS applications and virtual environments for military visualization and simulation.

Background

Dave spent the first 21 years of his life at and around Purdue University in West Lafayette, Indiana. He graduated from Purdue in 1994 with a B.S.E. in Systems Engineering and a B.S. in Computer Science . Dave then moved to Atlanta to work at the GVU Center, and intends to begin study soon on a Ph.D. in Computer Science.
