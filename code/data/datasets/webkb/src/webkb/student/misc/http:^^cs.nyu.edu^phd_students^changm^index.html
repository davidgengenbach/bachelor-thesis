Vita

My home country is Taiwan. I joined NYU in Fall of 1992 as a doctoral student of its Computer Science Department. I am currently working on my thesis project --- a divide-and-conquer reasoning and planning system on the card-playing phase of Bridge. I had my undergraduate study at the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University from 1984 to 1988. After receiving my B.S., I did a compulsory two-year military service, then I worked as a research assistant at the Institute of Information Science, Academic Sinica, from 1990-1992.
