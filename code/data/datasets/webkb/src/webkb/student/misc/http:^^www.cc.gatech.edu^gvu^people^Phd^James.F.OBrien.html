James F. O'Brien
I am a Doctoral Student in the College of Computing at the Georgia Institute of Technology, and a member of the Graphics, Visualization, and Usability Center. I spend most of my waking hours in the Animation Lab where I like to stare at computer monitors.

My primary area of research involves the physically based simulation of complex deformable systems to generate Secondary Motion for use in computer generated animation.

My wife, Heather.
