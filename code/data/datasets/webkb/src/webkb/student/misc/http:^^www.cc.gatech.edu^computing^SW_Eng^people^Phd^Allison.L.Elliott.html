Allison L. Elliott


Allison Elliott is a PhD student in the College of Computing at the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta . She is a member of the Software Systems Design Group (SSDG) and the Center for Information Management Research (CIMR).


Her research interests include software engineering , human-computer interaction, requirements engineering , computer-supported cooperative work, and Business Process Reengineering.


She received a B.S. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech in 1994.
