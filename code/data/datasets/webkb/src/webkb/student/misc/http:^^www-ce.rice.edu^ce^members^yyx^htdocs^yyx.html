YanYang Xiao

yyx@rice.edu

YanYang Xiao received her B.S. degree in physics from University of Science and Technology of China, China in 1990, and her M.A. degree in physics from Rice University, Houston, Texas in 1993. She is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at Rice University, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Her main interests are in shared memory parallel computer architectures, and optical network architecture.
