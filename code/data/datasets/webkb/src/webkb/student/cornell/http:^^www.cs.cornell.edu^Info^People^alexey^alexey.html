Alexey I. Vaysburd

alexey@cs.cornell.edu

I am in the Ph.D. Program in Computer Science at Cornell University .

My research interests include distributed systems, group communication, large-scale/wide-area systems, and object-oriented tools for distributed computing . I work within the Horus group lead by Ken Birman and Robbert van Renesse .

Papers:

  • Implementing Replicated State Machines Over Partitionable Networks. Roy Friedman and Alexey Vaysburd. Cornell University TR96-1581.
