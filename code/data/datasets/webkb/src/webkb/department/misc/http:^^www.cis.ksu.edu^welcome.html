Welcome to CIS at KSU! Kansas State University is located in Manhattan, Kansas, a short distance west of Kansas City. Our department lives in Nichols Hall (shown in the above picture), the newest/oldest building on campus. From its founding in 1967 to the present day, the CIS mission has been excellence in teaching, scholarship, and research. Visit our Web pages to see what we're doing!
