Department Overview
An overview of our department, where we are located, our administration, the College we are part of, the Library, and our local community. We are searching for new faculty members for Fall 97.
 
Programs
We offer B.A. and B.S. degrees in computer science as well as two graduate degrees: the M.S. and the Ph.D. We also participate in the Computational Science Cluster.
 
People
