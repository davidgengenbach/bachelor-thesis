Welcome to the home page of our ongoing MURI project being lead by Dr. T. Boult with team members (in alphabetic order):
  • Prof Peter Allen of Columbia University
  • Prof. Rick Blum of Lehigh University
  • Prof. John Kender of Columbia University
  • Prof. Roger Nagel of Lehigh University
  • Prof. Shree Nayar of Columbia University
  • Prof. Richard Wallace of New York University.

Currently selectable topics :

  • A textual summary of the project and its goals
  • A description of SLAM (Software Library for Appearance Matching) and its associated 100-object recognition system. This includes a video clip of it in action.
  • A description of the real-time shape-from-focus range sensor including a few video clips
  • Information on Spherical Pointing Motor-based camera/laser housing , or an 100K mpeg movie of a SPM moving a laser. The SPM is housing for the NYU
  • Information on Color/polarization, based processing and some of its applications.
  • Information on Model-based Sensor Planning (with movie).
  • Information on Laser-Range finder-based 3D model Acquisition and recognition
