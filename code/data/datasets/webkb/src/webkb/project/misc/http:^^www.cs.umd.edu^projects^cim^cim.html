The University of Maryland

Institute for Systems Research (ISR)

Welcome to CIM Group's Mosaic Homepage!

Purpose: To advance the state-of-the art in Computer Integrated Manufacturing, both at the factory and the shop floor level. Emphasis is given to the development and application of advanced modeling tools for complex manufacturing systems. The integration of information is being implemented using AI production systems and distributed architectures.

Support: NSF, NIST, Bentley Systems Incorporated, U.S. Army Tank and Automotive Command, Westinghouse ESG, Merck

Location: 0113 Engineering Research Building (093)

Research Areas:

  • Agile Manufacturing
  • Automated Generation of Redesign Suggestions for Machined Parts
  • Facility Design
  • Feature Recognition
  • Hierarchical Production Management Systems
  • Hybrid Facility Master Planning
  • The IMACS Project: A System for Automated Manufacturability Analysis and Design Critiquing.
  • Job Shop Scheduling
  • Mail Sorting
  • Manufacturability Evaluation
  • Petri Nets for Manufacturing Systems
  • Virtual Factories for Electro-Mechanical Device Manufacturing
