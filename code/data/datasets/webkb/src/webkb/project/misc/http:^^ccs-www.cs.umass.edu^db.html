Database Systems Laboratory
Department of Computer Science
University of Massachusetts, Amherst


The Database Systems Laboratory is part of the Computer Science Department at the University of Massachusetts. Our current research activities deal with enhancing performance and availability of applications that require transaction support through the use of semantic information about the objects, operations, transaction model, and the application.
