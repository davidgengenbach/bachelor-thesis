IC-2 Home Page

IC-2, titled "A Knowledge-Based and Object-Oriented Spatial Database System for GIS Applications" is an IRIS-PRECARN project in the theme of Intelligent Computation . Our gopher/WWWeb node is housed by the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University. Its purpose is to disseminate project information to project members, sponsoring organisations, and other researchers interested in this active research area.
