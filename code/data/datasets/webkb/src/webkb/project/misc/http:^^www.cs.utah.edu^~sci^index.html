** UNDER RENNOVATION ** (10/10/96)
The Scientific Computing and Imaging (SCI) research group in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Utah has ongoing research projects in many areas of scientific computing: geometric modeling, numerical analysis, parallel computing, scientific visualization, human-computer interaction, and software development tools. Our group is involved in both designing efficient and accurate tools for scientific computing, and in utilizing these tools for scientific applications.
