Sound Probabilistic Reasoning Project (SPR)

Primary Investigator: Eric Neufeld . The SPR project considers probabilistic approaches to a variety of AI problems traditionally handled by logics, grammars, or heuristics. Students in the past have look at approximate reasoning, natural language, reasoning about time, and classification of data.

Logic programming

Working jointly with Tony Kusalik , some students are investigating problems in logic programming, including abstract interpretation and visual unification.
