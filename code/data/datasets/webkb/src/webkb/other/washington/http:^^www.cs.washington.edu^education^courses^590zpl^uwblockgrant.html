How to Apply for an NCSA Block Grant Account

Any University of Washington faculty, staff or atudent may apply for a block grant account.

For the NCSA block grant application, your request must include all of the following information:

  • Principal investigator or instructor name
  • Number of service hours desired
  • Project title
  • Field of research
  • Full names, US mail addresses (including room and building) phone numbers, e-mail addresses, social security numbers, and university affiliation (i.e., undergrad, grad, etc.) of every user that would have access to the account.
You can e-mail this information to info@cac.washington.edu. They will send the information to San Diego and your account will be created.
