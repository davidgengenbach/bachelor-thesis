The Internet Learning Agent (ILA)

The number and diversity of information resources on the net is growing rapidly. To enable people to keep up with this growth and find information on the net, we propose the Internet Learning Agent, which forms models of information resources by interacting with them.

Papers:

  • Category Translation: Learning to understand information on the Internet has been published in IJCAI95 and the AAAI95 Spring Symposium on information gathering.
  • Learning to Understand Information on the Internet: An Example-Based Approach elaborates on ILA and includes the ShopBot. The paper will appear in the Journal of Intelligent Information Systems.

This is work by Mike Perkowitz and Oren Etzioni.
