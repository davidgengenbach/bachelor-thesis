The UW Internet Raquetball Ladder

Welcome to the University of Washington Internet Raquetball Ladder! The Ladder is an ongoing tournament played at the UW IMA building. If you are a UW student, faculty, or staff member, this is your chance to find players of your own skill level. It's fun, it's hip, and it's good for you, too! You don't need any experience...you don't even need your own racquet. All you need is killer instinct and about an hour a week. Come on and get in on the action!
