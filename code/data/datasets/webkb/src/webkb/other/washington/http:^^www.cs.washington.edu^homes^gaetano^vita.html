Gaetano Borriello's Vita as of January 1996

Research Interests

Computer-aided design of embedded systems; hardware/software co-design; digital system design; programmable logic architectures and tools; mobile computing; special-purpose architectures; user interfaces.

    • Visiting Scientist, Inter-University Microelectronics Center, Leuven, Belgium (1992)
  • University of California at Berkeley (Berkeley, CA) - Computer Science Division
    • Research Assistant (1984-1988)

  • Xerox Corporation (Palo Alto, CA) - Palo Alto Research Center
    • Member of Research Staff (1981-1984)

  • B.S. Electrical Engineering (Computer Engineering), Polytechnic Institute of New York, 1979

Professional Awards

  • Distinguished Teaching Award - University of Washington, 1995
  • Junior Faculty Achievement Award - College of Engineering, University of Washington, 1994
  • Presidential Young Investigator Award - National Science Foundation, 1988
