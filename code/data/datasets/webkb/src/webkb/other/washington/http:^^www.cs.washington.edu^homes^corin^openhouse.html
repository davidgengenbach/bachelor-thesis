Engineering Open House 1996

Computer Science and Engineering

Every other year, the College of Engineering at the University of Washington holds an Open House to encourage folks from the "Outside World" to take a peek at what's going on Inside. This year, the event was held April 19th and 20th.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, being part of the College of Engineering, hosted a handful of exhibits. In particular, CSE showed off its Graphics lab, PC lab, and Hardware Design lab, as well as sponsor several talks during both days.
