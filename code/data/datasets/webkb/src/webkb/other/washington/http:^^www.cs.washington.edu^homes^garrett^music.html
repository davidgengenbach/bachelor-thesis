Some musical notes

John Lee Hooker

John Lee Hooker has been playing blues guitar since the 1940's and he shows no signs of slowing down. His most recent albums, Chill Out, The Healer, Mr. Lucky feature more elaborate studio-produced arrangements with a variety of younger guest musicians. However, I also enjoy listening to his older recordings with small groups or playing solo.
