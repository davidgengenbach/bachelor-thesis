A different light governs the night hours. It influences our perceptions and our moods. It also reflects on our faces. This exhibit is dedicated to these other moments and their moods.
Twilight is best viewed with Netscape Navigator 2.0 or higher, with at least thousands of colors, and with the Toolbar, Location and Directory information switched off on smaller monitors.
