Ed's life

_ Unlike most of the people at the computer science department here, I was born in Seattle, and I have lived in Seattle for most of my life.

_ I have one brother, one sister, and a whole bunch of cousins, and an even bigger bunch of second cousins.

I went to Mount Rainier high school and was the 2nd graduating class to participate in the _ International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma program. (My older brother was in the first graduating class to be able to take IB classes. I went to Yale for my undergraduate degree (a B. S. in computer science and mathematics).
