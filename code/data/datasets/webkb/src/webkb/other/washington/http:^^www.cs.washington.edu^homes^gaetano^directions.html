From Seattle-Tacoma International Airport

Leave the main terminal area, following signs for "Freeways" and "Seattle". The six-lane airport entry road merges into SR518, and about 3/4 miles later, take a left exit for I-5 north and Seattle. Merge into I-5, and follow directions for I-5 (from south)

From I-5 (from south)

Pass the exit for Seattle-Tacoma International Airport, and continue north for about 6 miles, through downtown Seattle (do not take the express lanes). Pass the junction with SR520, and cross a high bridge, moving to the right hand lane. Follow signs for the University of Washington, taking the exit for 45th Street. Make sure you stay in the correct lane for a right (or East) turn onto 45th. Follow 45th for about 1 mile, crossing Roosevelt, Brooklyn, University, and 15th. Just beyond 15th, turn right into the UW campus, stopping at the parking booth. The attendant can give you a map of the campus and directions for parking. To reach Sieg Hall directly, left onto Stevens Way at the first intersection past the parking booth, and follow it for about 1/2 mile around the edge of campus, passing two stop signs. Immediately on the right after the second stop sign is the Husky Union Building (HUB). Once you pass the HUB you can make a right around the bus stop and proceed up to a small circle. Sieg Hall is the building directly opposite the HUB (you can't miss it if you look for an old Holiday Inn in need of renovation). There is no guest or visitor parking in the vicinity of the department, however. Parking in the circle is only practical for the very shortest stops.

From I-5 (from north)

From the north, follow I-5 south toward downtown Seattle. Do not use the express lanes (which may or may not be open>. Pass the Northgate shopping mall on your left, and exits for 85th and 65th streets. Move into right lanes, and take the 45th Street exit, staying on your left and then turning left onto 45th. Follow 45th for about 1 mile, crossing Roosevelt, Brooklyn, University, and 15th. Just beyond 15th, turn right into the UW campus, stopping at the parking booth. The attendant can give you a map of the campus and directions for parking.
