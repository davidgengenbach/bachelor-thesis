Janet Kolodner, Director

On January 1, 1993, the EduTech Institute was established at the Georgia Institute of Technology. EduTech serves as a coordinator and facilitator of efforts to improve the educational process at Georgia Tech. Wherever possible, EduTech will focus on the transfer of those improvements into pre-college environments.
