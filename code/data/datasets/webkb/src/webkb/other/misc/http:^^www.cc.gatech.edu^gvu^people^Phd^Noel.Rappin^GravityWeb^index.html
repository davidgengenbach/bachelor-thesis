Welcome To Gravity Web

GRAVITY was originally a Humor Magazine at Brandeis University. This web site is a collection of some of the best material that we printed during that run, as well as new material from the people that brought you GRAVITY in the first place.

Take a look around. We'll be adding new stuff all the time, so come back in a couple of weeks to look again.

GravityWeb is brought to you by Matthew A. Cohen, Ross Garmil, Noel Rappin, and Jason Schneider.

Thanks for stopping by.
