Lab Information

Lab accounts are available now. You can get an account by going to the undergraduate office and filling out the account form for the lab. They have a list of students in the class, and you will have to provide some personal data and sign the form. Accounts have preset login ids and passwords. Please note that these accounts will expire 2 weeks after the spring semester begins. If you will for any reason need the account to be up longer than that, I will have to request an extension.

The lab is currently open from 8 am to 12 midnight. Hours may be extended if necessary later in the semester. If these hours are a problem for you, please let me know in writing or by email.
