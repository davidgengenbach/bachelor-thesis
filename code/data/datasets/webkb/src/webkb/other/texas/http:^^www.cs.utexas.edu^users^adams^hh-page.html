Happy Hour

I'm Adam Seligman, a second year Masters student, and I currently run an informal happy hour that happens roughly every week during the year. Although I'm the GRACS Social Chair (and President of Vice, I suspect), this isn't an official CS Department or GRACS event. Students, faculty, staff, significant and insignificant others are all welcome. We usually get together on Friday evenings between 5-7pm, subject to my whim. Some people drink beer, others drink soda, we all eat chips and salsa and generally have a good time. I have a list of people interested, to whom I send a weekly mail telling you when and where to meet.

Drop me a note saying you'd like to be added to the list, and I'll let you know before each happy hour. It's a great way to get to know some of the people in the department you might not meet otherwise, and to get the scoop on the in's and out's of UT's CS program.

Adam Seligman
