RoboKreta

RoboKreta-Besar

RoboKreta-Kicik

RoboKreta-Besar and RoboKreta-Kicik are two autonomous intelligent toy cars built using the chassis and motors of two fast radio-controlled (RC) toy racing cars. Brains based on the MIT 6.270 and miniboard controller boards are placed in the cars. They are still under metamorphosis and they should be ready for their debut in upcoming Robofest.
