International Organization for Plant Information

The International Organization for Plant Information (IOPI) manages a series of cooperative international projects that aim to create databases of plant taxonomic information. IOPI is a Commission of the International Union of Biological Sciences (IUBS)

World Species List - Plant Searches

An inventory of over 140 searcheable plant databases -- many of them are gopher indices.

This is an alphabetically arranged list of international herbaria and living collections. These are large searchable databases which contain thousands of species.

World Wide Web Consortium

The W3 Consortium provides an veritable well-spring of information about the web. At this site, one can find standards documents for all aspects of the web.
