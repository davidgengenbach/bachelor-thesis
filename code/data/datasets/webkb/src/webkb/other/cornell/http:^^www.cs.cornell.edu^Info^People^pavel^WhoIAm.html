Who I Am

I am a Ph.D. student at the Departmant of Computer Science of Cornell University. I work with Professor Robert L. Constable. In Fall 1994 I maintained Web page of course in Advanced Programming Languages (CS611). This semester I start my work for Nuprl project.

I graduated from the Department of Mechanics and Mathematics of Moscow State University (Moscow , Russia). Under supervision of Professor S.Artemov I made several publications in Mathematical Logic.

My wife, Elena, now is a graduate students in Economics at the State University of New York at Buffalo. We are married more then 4 years and know each other more than 10 years because we graduated the same high school in Moscow.
