The MITRE Corporation

I have worked for The MITRE Corporation of McLean, Virginia for three summers. Most recently, I investigated ADS-B requirements under Free Flight. The project was funded by the Federal Aviation Administration.

Virginia Tech

I performed research with Dr. Bill Curtin of Virginia Tech. We investigated the affect of bridging arrangements on the R-curve and time-to-fracture of brittle ceramics. Our paper has been submitted for publication to the Journal of the American Ceramics Society.
