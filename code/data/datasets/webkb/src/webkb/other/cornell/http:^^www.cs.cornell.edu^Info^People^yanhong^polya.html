Composing Efficient Programs

Optimization and selection techniques for building effcient programs from components.

Theme

It is a trend that software be composed from existing components, forming even larger components. One of the most challenging issues is the efficiency of the resulting programs, which depends largely on optimization across component interfaces and selection of appropriate components given complicated interactions among them. How to perform such optimization and selection systematically is one of my particular interests.
