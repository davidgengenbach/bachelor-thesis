    Readings

      Paper Review Form

      Analog Video Representations

      • Blinn, James F., The World of Digital Video, IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications, September, 1992.
      • C. A. Poynton, Technical Introduction to Digital Video, John Wiley & Sons, 1995, pp 1-32
      • C. A. Poynton, Colour FAQ

      Video Compression

      • Wallace, Gregory The JPEG Still Picture Compression Standard IEEE Transactions on Consumer Electronics, Feb. 1992, vol.38,(no.1):xviii-xxxiv
      • Le Gall, Didier MPEG: A Video Compression Standard for Multimedia Applications Communications of the ACM, April 1991, vol.34, (no.4):46-58

      Audio Compression

      • A.J.M. Houtsma, Psychophysics and Modern Digital Audio Technology, Philips Journal Research, Vol 47, 1992, pp 3-14.
      • Zwicker, Eberhard and Zwicker, U. Tilmann Audio Engineering and Psychoacoustics: Matching Signals to the Final Receiver, The Human Auditory System Journal of the Audio Engineering Society, March 1991, vol.39, (no.3):115-26.

      Video Processing

      • B. C. Smith, L. A. Rowe Algorithms for Manipulating Compressed Images, IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications, September, 1993.
      • L. Tang, Methods for Encrypting and Decrypting MPEG Video Data Efficiently, Proc. of the Fourth ACM International Conference on Multimedia, Boston, MA, November, 1996.
      • T. Beier, S. Neely, Feature-Based Image Metamorphosis, Proc. of SIGGRAPH 92, July 1992, pp 35-42

      Video on Demand

      • D. J. Gemmel, et. al., Multimedia Storage Servers: A Tutorial IEEE Computer, May 1995
      • A. Reddy, Disk Scheduling in a Multimedia I/O System Proceedings of the First ACM Conference on Multimedia, San Francisco, CA, August 1-6, 1993, p. 225-234, ACM Press

      Networks

      • M. R. Macedonia, D. P. Brutzman, MBone Provides Audio and Video Across the Internet, IEEE Computer, Vol.27 #4, April 1994, pp. 30-36.
        ftp://taurus.cs.nps.navy.mil/pub/mbmg/mbone.html
      • B. C. Smith, Cyclic-UDP: A Priority Driven Best-Effort Protocol, /Info/Projects/zeno/Papers/cudp.pdf
      • L. Zhang, S. Deering, et. al., RSVP: A New Resource ReSerVation Protocol, IEEE Network, vol. 7, no. 5, pp. 8-18, September 1993.
        http://www.isi.edu/div7/rsvp/rsvp.html
