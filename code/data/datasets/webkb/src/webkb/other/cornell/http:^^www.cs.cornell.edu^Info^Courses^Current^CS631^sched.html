CS631 Project Presentation Schedule

All presentations will take place in 5135. Presentations will last 15 minutes, with 5 minutes for questions. The presentations are open to faculty, students, staff, the homeless, and roaming animals. In other words, everyone is welcome. We'll have an overhead projector, a VCR/TV, a Win 95 machine, and a projection screen available.

A list of project proposals is available here.
