Research

I am working on program testing. My advisor is Ronitt Rubinfeld. For more information on program testing, Hal Wasserman has a testing page in Berkeley that also contains links to some of the people who have done work in the field. I am also interested in Computational Learning Theory and Cryptography.

Publications

  • On Testing Without the Generator Bottleneck. With S. R. Kumar, D. Sivakumar. Submitted to SIAM Journal of Computing.

  • Approximate Checking of Polynomials and Functional Equations With S. R. Kumar, R. Rubinfeld, FOCS 1996.

  • On Learning Bounded-Width Branching Programs With S. R. Kumar, R. Rubinfeld, COLT 1995.

  • Testing Multivariate Linear Functions: Overcoming the Generator Bottleneck STOC 1995.
