  • House Of Games, Things Change (both David Mamet)
  • Anything by Joel & Ethan Coen
  • The Fabulous Baker Boys
  • Annie Hall
  • Real Genius
  • 29th Street
  • Fearless
  • Monty Python's Life of Brian
