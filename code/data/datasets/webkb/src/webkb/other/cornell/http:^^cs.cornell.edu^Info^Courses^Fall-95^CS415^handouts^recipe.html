Penne ai broccoli

PENNE AI BROCCOLI: pasta; sauce; pecorino cheese.
  • Add the sauce to the pasta.
  • Grate fresh pecorino cheese (not the pre-grated stuff!) on top of it.
  • Buon appetito!

PASTA: water; salt; penne; broccoli.
  • Bring water to boil; add salt, broccoli florets, and penne.
  • Stir well. Drain when al dente (about 10 minutes, if you bought a good brand of pasta).
SAUCE: extra-vergin olive oil; garlic; chili peppers.
  • In extra vergin olive oil, brown 3 or 4 cloves of garlic and a couple of chili peppers.
  • Remove cloves and peppers before serving.
