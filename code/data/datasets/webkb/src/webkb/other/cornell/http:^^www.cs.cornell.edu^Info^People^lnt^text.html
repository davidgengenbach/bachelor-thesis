NUMERICAL LINEAR ALGEBRA

Lloyd N. Trefethen and David Bau, III
xii+355 pages
SIAM, 1997

David Bau (currently at Microsoft) and I have recently completed a graduate textbook on numerical linear algebra. Our aims in this book are beauty, depth of insight, and brevity. The text is split into forty lectures, each about eight pages long. It is based on the course I have taught repeatedly at MIT and Cornell during the past ten years.

The book is presently undergoing copy editing by SIAM, and it will be published in 1997.
