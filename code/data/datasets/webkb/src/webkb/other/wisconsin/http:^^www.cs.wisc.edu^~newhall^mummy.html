Mummies from Museo de las Momias in Guanajuato Mexico

These are some pictures from my trip to the Mummy Museum in Guanajuato. The mummies were displayed behind glass cases. Each case had a small hole in one of its corners, and as a result the museum smelled like mummy (the smell was the most disturbing thing about the mummies).
