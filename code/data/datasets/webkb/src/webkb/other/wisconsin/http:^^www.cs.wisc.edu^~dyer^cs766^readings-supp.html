CS 766 Supplementary Reading Sources


  • Image Processing Textbooks
    • R. C. Gonzalez and R. E. Woods, Digital Image Processing, Addison-Wesley, Reading, Mass., 1992.
    • J. C. Russ, The Image Processing Handbook, CRC Press, Boca Raton, Fl., 1992.
    • W. K. Pratt, Digital Image Processing, 2nd ed., Wiley, New York, 1991.
    • A. K. Jain, Fundamentals of Digital Image Processing, Prentice Hall, Englewood Cliffs, N.J., 1989.

  • Computer Vision Textbooks
    • D. H. Ballard and C. M. Brown, Computer Vision, Prentice-Hall, Englewood Cliffs, N.J., 1982.
    • R. M. Haralick and L. G. Shapiro, Computer and Robot Vision, Vols. I and II, Addison-Wesley, Reading, Mass., 1992.
    • V. S. Nalwa, A Guided Tour of Computer Vision, Addison-Wesley, Reading, Mass., 1993.
    • B. K. P. Horn, Robot Vision, McGraw-Hill, New York, 1986.
    • O. Faugeras, Three-Dimensional Computer Vision, MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass., 1993.

  • Computer Vision Monographs
    • D. Marr, Vision, Freeman, San Francisco, 1982.
    • J. Aloimonos and D. Shulman, Integration of Visual Modules: An Extension of the Marr Paradigm, Academic Press, New York, 1989.
    • J. Koenderink, Solid Shape, MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass., 1990.
    • W. E. L. Grimson, Object Recognition by Computer: The Role of Geometric Constraints, MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass., 1990.
    • B. A. Wandell, Foundations of Vision, Sinauer, Sunderland, Mass., 1995.

  • Paper Collections: General
    • M. Fischler and O. Firschein, eds., Readings in Computer Vision, Morgan Kaufmann, Los Altos, Ca., 1987.
    • R. Kasturi and R. C. Jain, eds., Computer Vision: Principles, and Computer Vision: Advances and Applications, IEEE Computer Society Press, Los Alamitos, Ca., 1991.

  • Paper Collections: Special Topics
    • C. M. Brown and D. Terzopoulos, eds., Real-time Computer Vision, Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 1994.
    • J. L. Mundy and A. Zisserman, eds., Geometric Invariance in Computer Vision, MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass., 1992.
    • Y. Aloimonos, ed., Active Perception, Lawrence Erlbaum, Hillsdale, N.J., 1993.
    • A. Blake and A. Yuille, eds., Active Vision, MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass., 1992.
    • L. B. Wolff, S. A. Shafer and G. E. Healey, eds., Physics-Based Vision: Principles and Practice (in three volumes: Shape Recovery, Radiometry, and Color), Jones and Bartlett, Boston, 1992.
    • A. K. Jain and P. J. Flynn, eds., Three-Dimensional Object Recognition Systems, Elsevier, Amsterdam, 1993.

  • Papers
    • Sensors
      • J. Blinn, The world of digital video, IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications 12(5), 1992, 106-112.
      • J. Blinn, NTSC: Nice technology, super color, IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications 13(2), 1993, 17-23.
      • P. Besl, Active, optical range image sensors, Machine Vision and Applications 1(2), 1988, 127-152.
      • S. Sinha and R. Jain, Range image analysis, in Handbook of Pattern Recognition and Image Processing: Computer Vision, T. Young, ed., Academic Press, New York, 1994, 185-237.
    • Image Processing
    • Human-Computer Interface
      • F. Quek, Eyes in the interface, Image and Vision Computing 13(6), 1995, 511-525.
      • C. Tomasi and L. Guibas, Image Descriptions for Browsing and Retrieval, Proc. Image Understanding Workshop, 1994.
      • W. Freeman and M. Roth, Orientation Histograms for Hand Gesture Recognition, Proc. Int. Workshop Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition, 1995.
      • W. Freeman and C. Weissman, Television control by hand gestures, Proc. Int. Workshop Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition, 1995.
      • J. Rehg and T. Kanade, DigitEyes: Vision-Based Human Hand Tracking, CMU TR CMU-CS-93-220, 1993.
      • R. Cipolla, et al., Uncalibrated Stereo Vision with Pointing for a Man-Machine Interface, Machine Vision and Applications, 1994. (file called cipolla_mva94.ps.Z)
      • A. Gee and R. Cipolla, Non-Intrusive Gaze Tracking for Human-Computer Interaction, Proc. Mechantronics and Machine Vision in Practice, 1994, 112-117 (file called gee_mmvip.ps.Z).
      • A. Gee and R. Cipolla, Determining the Gaze of Faces in Images, University of Cambridge Technical Report CUED/F-INFENG/TR 174, 1994 (file called gee_tr174.ps.Z).
      • A. Gee and R. Cipolla, Fast Visual Tracking by Temporal Consensus, University of Cambridge Technical Report CUED/F-INFENG/TR 207, 1995 (file called gee_tr207.ps.Z).
      • Y. Yacoob, H. Lam, and L. Davis, Recognizing Faces showing Expressions, Proc. Int. Workshop on Gesture and Face Recognition, 1995. (Plus see other papers by Yacoob on the web.)
    • Mosaics
      • R. Szeliski, Image mosaicing for tele-reality applications, DEC CRL Technical Report 94/2, 1994.
      • R. Szeliski and S. Kang, Direct methods for visual scene reconstruction, Proc. Workshop on Representation of Visual Scenes, 1995, to appear.
      • R. Kumar, P. Anandan, M. Irani, J. Bergen, and K. Hanna, Representation of scenes from collections of images, Proc. Workshop on Representation of Visual Scenes, 1995, to appear.
      • M. Hansen, P. Anandan, K. Dana, G. van der Wal, and P. Burt, Real-time scene stabilization and mosaic construction, Proc. Image Understanding Workshop, 1994, 457-465.
      • P. Burt and P. Anandan, Image stabilization by registration to a reference mosaic, Proc. Image Understanding Workshop, 1994, 425-434.
      • M. Irani and S. Peleg, Improving resolution by image registration, Computer Vision, Graphics, and Image Processing: Graphical Models and Image Processing 53, 1991, 231-239.
      • S. Chen, QuickTime VR - An image-based approach to virtual environment navigation, Proc. SIGGRAPH '95, 1995, 29-38.
      • L. McMillan and G. Bishop, Plenoptic modeling: An image-based rendering system, Proc. SIGGRAPH '95, 1995, 39-46.
      • L. McMillan, Acquiring immersive virtual environments with an uncalibrated camera, University of North Carolina Technical Report TR95-006, 1995.
      • S. Mann and R. Picard, Video orbits of the projective group: A new perspective on image mosaicing, MIT Media Lab Technical Report TR-338, 1995.

      • S. Chen and L. Williams, View interpolation for image synthesis, Proc. SIGGRAPH '93, 1993, 279-288.
      • S. Seitz and C. Dyer, Physically-valid view synthesis by image interpolation, Proc. Workshop on Representation of Visual Scenes, 1995, to appear.
