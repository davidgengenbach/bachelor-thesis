Directions to My Office

To get to CS&S 3310, walk into the building's front door on Dayton St. and keep walking straight until you see the large Computer Sciences Department photo board. Go through the door to the right of the photo board and take the stairs up one-and-a-half floors. You will see a door with a piece of paper taped to it that says 3310. Go through that door and follow the hallway around the corner until you see a large fishbowl-like office. My desk is in this office; it's the one with the bumper sticker on the monitor.

For visual directions to 3310, try Tom Hogan's virtual reality map.
