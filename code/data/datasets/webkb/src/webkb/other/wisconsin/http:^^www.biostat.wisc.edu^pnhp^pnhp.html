Welcome to the PNHP web site at the University of Wisconsin, Madison Medical School! This site is designed to be a primer on aspects of the U.S. health care system, and provide information about single-payer health care reform.

The Physicians for a National Health Program is a national organization representing more than 7000 physicians, health care planners, students and concerned individuals. We support health care reform for the United States, proposing a single-payer system. The official web site for the national PNHP is http://www.pnhp.org.
