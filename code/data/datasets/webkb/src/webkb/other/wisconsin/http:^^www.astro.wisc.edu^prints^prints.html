Electronic Prints

The following articles are offered by the authors in several possible formats. Postscript and LaTeX files can be downloaded to your local disk by choosing "Save Next Link..." or an equivalent command in your browser. Shift-clicking on a link will work in some window-based browsers. Postscript files may also be viewed on-screen with a Postscript previewer (such as Ghostview) if your browser is set up properly. Astronomical LaTeX papers usually require the AASTeX LaTeX macro package to compile. Finally, Links may lead you to more options or even to the actual article.
