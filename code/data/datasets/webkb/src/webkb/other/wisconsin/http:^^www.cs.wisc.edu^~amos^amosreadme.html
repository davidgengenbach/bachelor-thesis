Selected articles of Amos Ron

all icons on this page are clickable

each article listed below can be viewed on-line by clicking the red ball next to it. Or course, all articles are downloadable. They can also ftp'ed from ftp.cs.wisc.edu/Approx, but that's a silly option, if you are already here. The files are postscript, and are also available as compress(ed) files, as indicated by the subscript .Z, to be uncompress(ed) before using.
The files are in order of increasing age.
