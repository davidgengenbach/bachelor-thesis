What's "longhair music"?

From Websters': 

   long.hair n  \'lonj-.ha(*)r, -.he(*)r\
    [back-formation fr. longhaired]
      1 : a person of artistic gifts or interests; esp : 
          a lover of classical music
      2 : an impractical intellectual
    long-hair adj or long-haired

Classical, intellectual, impractical...yeah, I like to listen to Gustav Mahler! Other favorite composers this week include Rachmaninoff, J.S. Bach, Handel, and Chopin.

Recent listening

  • Ella at Duke's Place! A swinging respite from long-haired music. What could be better for a Saturday afternoon? Ella is the Queen of Scat!
  • Brahms: Handel Variations, piano.
  • Johan de Meij: Symphony No. 1 for Wind Band, "The Lord of the Rings." Romantic, brassy, cosmic. Reminds me of a cross between Wagner and John Williams, only better.
  • Mahler: Symphony No. 9, Bruno Walter, 1961.
