CS 736 Spring 1995
Advanced Operating Systems
Reading List

This is the list of readings for CS 736, and the approximate order in which we will read them. Changes to this list are possible during the course of the semester. A tentative schedule is provided below.

    Synchronization and Communication

  1. E. W. Dijkstra, ``Hierarchical ordering of sequential processes'', in Operating Systems Techniques, C. A. R. Hoare and R. H. Perrott (editors), Academic Press, London, 1972, 72-93. Also Acta Informatica 1 (1971), pp 115-138.
  2. G. R. Andrews and F. B. Schneider, ``Concepts and Notations for Concurrent Programming'', Computing Surveys 15, 1 (March 1983), 3-44.
  3. B. W. Lampson and D. D. Redell, ``Experiences with Processes and Monitors in Mesa'', Comm. of the ACM 23, 2 (February 1980), 105-117.
  4. B. D. Marsh, M. L. Scott, T. J. LeBlanc and E. P. Markatos, ``First-Class User-Level Threads'', SOSP13, , 110-121.
  5. H. Massalin and C. Pu, ``Threads and Input/Output in the Synthesis Kernel'', SOSP12, , 191-201.
  6. H. C. Lauer and R. M. Needham, ``On the Duality of Operating System Structures'', Operating Systems Review 13, 2 (April 1979), 3-19. Originally in Proc. 2nd Int'l Symposium on Operating Systems, IRIA, October 1978.

    Memory Organization and Management

  7. A. Bensoussan, C. T. Clingen and R. C. Daley, ``The Multics Virtual Memory: Concepts and Design'', Comm. of the ACM 15, 5 (May 1972), 308-318.
  8. R. S. Fabry, ``Capability-Based Addressing'', Comm. of the ACM 17, 7 (July 1974), 403-412.
  9. P. J. Denning, ``The Working Set Model of Program Behavior'', Comm. of the ACM 11, 5 (May 1968), 323-333.
  10. R. Carr and J. Hennessy, ``WSCLOCK-A Simple and Effective Algorithm for Virtual Memory Management'', SOSP8, , 87-95.
  11. O. Babaoglu and W. Joy, ``Converting a Swap-Based System to do Paging in an Architecture Lacking Page-Reference Bits'', SOSP8, , 78-86.
  12. M. Young, A. Tevanian, R. Rashid, D. Golub, J. Eppinger, J. Chew, W. Bolosky, D. Black and R. Baron, ``The Duality of Memory and Communication in the Implementation of a Multiprocessor Operating System'', SOSP11, , 63-76.

    Case Studies and Experiences

  13. H. C. Lauer, ``Observations on the Development of an Operating System'', SOSP8, , 30-36.
  14. B. W. Lampson, ``Hints for Computer System Design'', SOSP9, , 33-48.

    File Systems

  15. M. K. McKusick, W. N. Joy, S. J. Leffler and R. S. Fabry, ``A Fast File System for UNIX'', Trans. Computer Systems 2, 3 (August 1984), 181-197.
  16. M. Rosenblum and J. K. Ousterhout, ``The Design and Implementation of a Log-Structured File System'', SOSP13, , 1-15.
  17. E. Levy and A. Silberschatz, ``Distributed File Systems: Concepts and Examples'', Computing Surveys 22, 4 (December 1990), 321-374.

    Protection and Security

  18. A. K. Jones and W. A. Wulf, ``Towards the Design of Secure Systems'', Software-Practice & Experience 5, 4 (1975), 321-336.
  19. F. T. Gramp and R. H. Morris, ``UNIX Operating System Security'', AT&T Bell Laboratories Technical Journal 63, 8 (October 1984), 1649-1672.
  20. G. J. Popek and C. S. Kline, ``Encryption and Secure Computer Networks'', Computing Surveys 11, 4 (December 1979), 331-356.
  21. J. G. Steiner, C. Neuman and J. I. Schiller, ``Kerberos: An Authentication Service for Open Network Systems'', USENIX '88, Dallas, TX, February 1988, 191-202.

    Distributed Systems

  22. A. D. Birrell, R. Levin, R. M. Needham and M. D. Schroeder, ``Grapevine: An Exercise in Distributed Computing'', Comm. of the ACM 25, 4 (April 1982), 260-274.
  23. A. D. Birrell and B. J. Nelson, ``Implementing Remote Procedure Calls'', Trans. Computer Systems 2, 1 (February 1984), 39-59.

    Interesting Kernels

  24. R. Pike, D. L. Presotto, K. Thompson and H. Trickey, ``Plan 9 from Bell Labs'', Proceedings of the 1990 USENIX Conference, .
  25. D. R. Cheriton and W. Zwaenepoel, ``The Distributed V Kernel and its Performance for Diskless Workstations'', SOSP9, , 128-139.
  26. G. Hamilton and P. Kougiouris, ``The Spring Nucleus: A Microkernel for Objects'', 1993 Summer USENIX Conference, Cincinatti, June 1993, 3-44.
