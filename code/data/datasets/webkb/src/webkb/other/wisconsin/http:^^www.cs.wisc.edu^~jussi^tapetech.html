    DAT (4mm) Technology

    Overview

    First generation DAT (DDS-1 (Digital Data Storage)) drives store 2.0 GB per tape and transfer 0.183 MB/s. With 2:1 data compression, the capacity is 4.0 GB and speed is 0.366 MB/s.

    Second generation DAT (DDS-2) drives store 4.0 GB per tape and transfer 0.366 MB/s uncompressed. With 2:1 compression, the capacity is 8.0 GB and speed is 0.732 MB/s.

    DAT Media

    A DDS-2 cartridge costs $15-20.
